# Payment & Invoicing Policy

## Accepted methods
We accept major credit and debit cards, PayPal, and store credit. Payment is taken
at the time the order is placed.

## Failed payments
If a payment fails, the order is held for 24 hours to allow re-payment before it is
automatically cancelled.

## Invoices
An invoice is emailed automatically once an order is confirmed. Customers can also
download invoices from the Orders page. Invoice corrections can be requested within
60 days of purchase.

## Double charges
If a customer is charged twice for one order, the duplicate charge is refunded
within 5 business days once verified.
