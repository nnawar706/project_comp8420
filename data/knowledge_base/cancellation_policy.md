# Order Cancellation Policy

## Before dispatch
Orders can be cancelled free of charge any time before they are dispatched, from
the Orders page. A full refund is issued to the original payment method.

## After dispatch
Once dispatched, an order cannot be cancelled but can be returned under the Returns
Policy once delivered.

## Cancellation fees
There is no cancellation fee for standard orders cancelled before dispatch.
Custom or personalised orders already in production may incur a fee covering work done.
