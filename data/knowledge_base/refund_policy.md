# Refund Policy

## Eligibility
Refunds are available within 30 days of delivery for unused items in original
packaging. Items must be returned before a refund is issued, except for damaged
or faulty goods.

## Processing time
Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment
method. Bank processing may add a further 3-5 business days.

## Non-refundable items
Gift cards, perishable goods, and personalised items are non-refundable unless
faulty.

## Shipping-fee refunds
Original shipping fees are refunded only when the return is due to our error
(wrong or faulty item) or a delivery delayed beyond 7 business days.
