# Account & Subscription Policy

## Account management
Customers can update their email, password, delivery address, and contact details
from Account Settings. Address changes apply to future orders only.

## Newsletter
Customers can subscribe or unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time from Account
Settings or via the unsubscribe link in any email. Changes take effect immediately.

## Subscriptions
Recurring subscriptions can be paused or cancelled before the next billing date.
Cancelling stops future charges; it does not refund the current period.

## Account closure
Customers can request account deletion; personal data is removed within 30 days in
line with our privacy policy, except records we must keep for legal/accounting reasons.
